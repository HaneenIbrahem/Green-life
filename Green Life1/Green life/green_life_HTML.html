About Us

Welcome to green life restaurant

At Green Life the goal is simple, provide the community with healthy, nutritious and delicious options that allow us all to Eat Well and Live Well. Founded in 2008, Green Life is a family owned business. The brothers created Green Life as a health conscious destination focusing equally on nutrition and taste. We are dedicated to addressing food insecurity for homebound seniors and other vulnerable neighbors. We provide food and pantry items to those in need, including thousands of nutritious meals prepared in our onsite kitchen each week.
